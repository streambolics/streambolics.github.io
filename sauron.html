# Home to sa.uron.be This is my home page. Since I currently have nothing important to say, it might look a little bit ... er ... empty. ## How it works My home page is hosted on [GitHub Pages](http://pages.gihub.com). I author it in [MarkDown](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markdown), which is converted on the fly to appropriate HTML by a tool named [StrapDown JS](http://strapdownjs.com/). By the way, that conversion script runs on your computer, and I am therefore using a little bit of your computing power for my own purposes. And I'm not ashamed at all.. The DNS server is still at OVH, but it redirects here, since they don't want to host my pages anymore...
